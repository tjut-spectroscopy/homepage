THE SPECTROSCOY GROUP

The goal of the Xiong group is to observe ultrafast processes in nanomaterials at their spatial and temporal limits by developing and applying new spectroscopic techniques. To achieve this goal, the group is combining concepts and techniques from chemistry and physics, including multidimensional spectroscopy, surface specific spectroscopy, and high harmonic generation.
